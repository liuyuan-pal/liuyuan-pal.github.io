Ph.D. Student at HKU

yuanly@connect.hku.hk

I'm currently a final-year PhD student at HKU advised by Prof. Wenping Wang and Prof. Taku Komura. In 2018-2019, I worked in CAD&CG lab of Zhejiang University advised by Prof. Xiaowei Zhou. I received my master degree at LIESMARS of Wuhan University advised by Prof. Bisheng Yang. My research interest includes computer graphics and 3D computer vision. I currently work on topics about 3D AIGC including neural rendering/representations and diffusion generative models.

I'll graduate before Jul. 2024 and I'm looking for an academic position to continue doing research on CG and 3D CV (Diffusion/NeRF/NeuS/GS)! (Hong Kong is preferred.)
