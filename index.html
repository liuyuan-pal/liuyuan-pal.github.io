Yuan Liu (刘缘)

Incoming Assistant Professor@HKUST, PhD@HKU

yuanly@connect.hku.hk

I'm visiting NTU as a PostDoc working with Prof. Ziwei Liu. I obtained my PhD degree at HKU advised by Prof. Wenping Wang and Prof. Taku Komura. I'm one of the organizers of AnySyn3D. My research mainly concentrates on 3D vision and graphics. I currently work on topics about 3D AIGC including neural rendering, neural representations, and 3D generative models.

[Github]   [Twitter]   [Google Scholar]

Openings

I will join ISD HKUST as an assistant professor in 2025 spring. I'm going to recruit PhD students (start from 2025 fall), research assistants, and visiting students. We will focus on utilizing neural rendering/representations (NeRF, NeuS, 3D GS), 3D Generative Models and 3D LLMs for solving the following tasks.

  1. AI for 3D Asset Generation.
  2. 3D/4D Video Generation and Editing.
  3. City-scale 3D Reconstruction and Analysis.

If you are interested in working with me on these topics, please fill the Google form here and drop an email to yuanly@connect.hku.hk. I have experience in mentoring master or junior PhD students. Refer to the Mentoring section for more details.
